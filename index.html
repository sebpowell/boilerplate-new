Going on its 4th year, JAM provides a fantastic opportunity to expose your brand to a talented audience of some of London’s leading product managers, designers and founders / leading product teams

We are looking for great companies that want to help us run an exceptional event – one that inspires, energises and educates/elevates/cultivates the best product teams in town.

And we want you in the spotlight – this is why we’ve designed an open sponsorship menu, leaving it to you to decide what suits you best / how you’d like to get involved.

If you wish to stand out from the crowd with a bespoke experience, of if you have an idea or concept you wish to discuss, just get in touch. We’ll work together to bring you the best results!
